# Asset attribution

## head.glb — "Lee Perry-Smith" head

`head.glb` is the **Lee Perry-Smith** head scan, by **Lee Perry-Smith /
Infinite-Realities** (https://ir-ltd.net/). It is long distributed with three.js
(`examples/models/gltf/LeePerrySmith/LeePerrySmith.glb`) and is licensed
**Creative Commons Attribution (CC-BY)**.

Used here as the sculpted base for the procedural `face` (the `headMesh` option):
the engine seats the procedural eyes in its sockets and a fedora on the crown.

> Head model by Lee Perry-Smith (Infinite-Realities), licensed CC-BY.

## CesiumMan.glb

`CesiumMan.glb` is the Cesium Man sample model from the glTF-Sample-Models
repository (Cesium / Khronos), used as the animated dancer.
